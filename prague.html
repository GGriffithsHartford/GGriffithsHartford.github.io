ANGELA TRAVELS

WHEN TO GO

May and September are the best months to visit Prague. The weather is pleasant and there are fewer crowds than in summer. However, if you're looking for cheaper accommodation prices, consider visiting in the winter.

WHERE TO GO

1. See the Infant Jesus of Prague

2. Explore the Old Town Square

3. Watch the Astronomical Clock Strike an Hour

HOW TO GET THERE

The international Václav Havel Prague Airport is located on the northwestern outskirts of Prague in the Ruzyně district. Connections to the city center are provided by public transport buses, AE - Airport Express buses, or Tick Tack-Airport Taxi.
